What Is Lorem Ipsum and Why Use It

Lorem Ipsum is scrambled, nonsensical Latin-derived text that's been the standard placeholder content in design and publishing since the 1500s — long before digital design existed. Its origin traces back to a passage from Cicero's "de Finibus Bonorum et Malorum," deliberately scrambled to remove actual meaning.

Designers and developers use placeholder text instead of real content because it lets you evaluate layout, typography, and visual flow without the distraction of meaningful words pulling your attention toward content review instead of design review.

When to Use Placeholder Text

  • Wireframing and mockups — visualizing layout before final copy is written.
  • Font and typography testing — seeing how a typeface looks at scale without being influenced by what the words say.
  • Print layout testing — checking how text flows around images or fills columns before final content is ready.
  • Client presentations — showing a design concept without needing final approved copy first.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Lorem Ipsum mean anything? ▼
No — while it's derived from real Latin text (a passage from Cicero), it has been deliberately scrambled and altered to the point of having no coherent meaning. It's specifically designed to look like natural language text without conveying any actual message.
Should I use Lorem Ipsum in a final, published design? ▼
No — Lorem Ipsum should always be replaced with real content before publishing. It's strictly a placeholder for the design process; shipping it in a live website or document looks unprofessional and has occasionally happened by accident in real published work.
Are there alternatives to Lorem Ipsum? ▼
Yes — some designers prefer "greek text" alternatives themed around specific industries (like "Bacon Ipsum" or "Hipster Ipsum") for more engaging client presentations, though they serve the same fundamental placeholder purpose as traditional Lorem Ipsum.
